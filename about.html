The Official Store

Oliver Samuels is Jamaica’s most beloved comedian — a stage and screen icon who has been making the world laugh for over half a century.

From the legendary Oliver at Large to sold-out theatre runs across the Caribbean, the UK, and North America, his warmth and wit have crossed generations and borders. This store brings that same spirit into a collection you can wear.

Every piece is designed in the colours of home — clean black and white with the green and gold that run through everything Jamaican. Made well, made to last, and made with one love.
